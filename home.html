Why FreshSense?

A New Way to Protect Mushroom Farms

Mushrooms are sensitive and require a stable environment. Even small disruptions can lead to mold, bacteria, or unwanted smells that destroy harvests. FreshSense uses advanced odor-detection technology to catch these problems early, helping farmers protect their crops before anything goes wrong.

What Is FreshSense?

FreshSense is a smart device that continuously monitors the air in mushroom farms. It detects unusual odor patterns associated with mold, bacteria, or substrate issues and sends instant alerts to the farmer's mobile app.

Why Smell Matters

Before contamination becomes visible, fungi and bacteria release odor particles. Humans can’t detect them, but FreshSense can — giving farmers a valuable early warning.

How FreshSense Works

  • Smart Odor Sensor: Reads air quality every second.
  • Mobile App: Sends alerts like “Possible mold in Room 2.”
