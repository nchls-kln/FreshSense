A New Way to Protect Mushroom Farms

In the world of farming, mushrooms are special. They grow fast, they are healthy, and they are becoming more popular around the world. But mushroom growing also comes with a big problem: they are very sensitive. A small change in their environment can cause mold, bad bacteria, or unwanted smells that can ruin the entire harvest.

FreshSense uses smell-detection technology paired with smart software to help mushroom farmers protect their crops before anything goes wrong. It turns the natural power of smell into a tool that keeps farms safer, cleaner, and more profitable.

What Is FreshSense?

FreshSense is a simple device that “smells” the air inside mushroom farms. It detects unusual odors that may signal problems such as mold, bacteria, or substrate issues. When something is detected, FreshSense sends an alert to the farmer’s phone so action can be taken immediately.

Why Smell Matters

Before mold becomes visible or bacteria spreads, they release tiny odor particles. Humans cannot detect these early signals, but FreshSense can. This gives farmers a head start, allowing them to prevent contamination before it becomes harmful.

How FreshSense Works

1. Smart Odor Sensor

A small device placed in grow rooms reads the air every second. It checks for odors known to be early signs of trouble.

2. Mobile App

The app provides live readings and alerts such as:

  • “Warning: Possible mold activity in Room 2.”
  • “Alert: Air quality changing — check humidity or ventilation.”
